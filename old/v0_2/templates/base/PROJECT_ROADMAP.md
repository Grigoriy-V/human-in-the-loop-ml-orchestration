## Operating rules

1. The supervisor reads this file before selecting or changing project work.
2. Work proceeds one approved step at a time.
3. The human authorizes execution with a clear start, continue, or follow-plan
   command.
4. Agents do not silently skip, merge, expand, reorder, or invent steps.
5. A completed step records result, evidence, decision, and limitation.
6. Long, destructive, external, expensive, or otherwise consequential actions
   keep their explicit human gate.
